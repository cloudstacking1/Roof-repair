What is Roof Leak Repair?

Roof Leak Repair is the process of identifying and fixing leaks in a roof to prevent water damage and structural issues. Leaks can occur due to damaged shingles, cracked flashing, clogged gutters, or aging materials. The repair process typically involves locating the source of the leak, sealing or replacing damaged roofing components, and ensuring proper water drainage. Prompt roof leak repair helps prevent mold growth, wood rot, and costly interior damage.
